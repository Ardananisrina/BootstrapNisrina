Exo is a south Korean-Chinese boy band based Seoul, with nine members: Suho, Xiumin, lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O, Kai, and Sehun. The band was formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012.
